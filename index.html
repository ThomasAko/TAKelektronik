Our Story . . .

New and refurbished EU phones and tablets

Phones are indispensable tools for everyday life as we know it today. This is especially true in Africa where studies have shown that unprecedented number of people on the continent are connecting to the internet on their phones. Interestingly though, most of these phones are feature phones. Smartphone users are the minority on the continent. Well the thing is smartphones are expensive; the latest models are getting even more expensive. Just think iPhone X or Samsung Galaxy Note 8.

We started TAK elektronik after realizing how difficult it is for people in West Africa to find a reliable partner who can provide large volumes of quality smartphones. We are based in Belgium, meaning we work in a time zone compatible with many African countries. We speak English and French. We are committed to make sure African consumers can also enjoy the latest trends in smartphone technology. Great service every time is for our customers our lovely continent is what we are about.

We prefer to sell in bulk (several) devices. However, we also do retail sales. Get in touch by WhatsApp (+32 491 46 46 91) or e-mail (sales@takelektronik.com) to get a free quote and eventually place your order. We always advice our customers to get a precise idea of the specification of the phone(s) and/or tablet(s) they wish to buy. You can check the specification of any phone here.

After accepting your order, we issue a proforma invoice. In addition to the cost of your devices, the proforma invoice also includes shipping costs. All our prices are quoted in Euros (€) and are exclusive of value added tax (VAT) - buyers based outside Europe need not worry about VAT. You can check the exchange rate of the Euro with respect to you home currency here.
